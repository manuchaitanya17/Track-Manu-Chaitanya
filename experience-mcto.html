Integration Surface

Routes Visible in the M CTO Package

The file names alone show that M CTO is not one narrow integration. It covers entity creation, finance transactions, attachments, and payment-side processing.

Customer + Vendor

Create-customer, create-vendor, and customer-vendor pairing routes are present.

Sales + Purchase

Sales Order and Purchase Order creation RESTlets are included.

Finance Documents

Invoice, Vendor Bill, and Credit Memo creation routes widen the surface into finance operations.

Payments

Deposit, customer payment, refund payment, and vendor payment flows are present.

Attachment Support

A dedicated attach-PDF helper is included in the live source package.

Request Logging

Request-object tracking is built directly into the create-invoice flow.
