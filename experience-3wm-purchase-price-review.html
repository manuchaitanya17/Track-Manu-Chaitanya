Control Surface

What the 3WM Package Actually Contains

The 3WM archive is the most complete control framework in this experience tree because it spans custom records, validation, scheduled processing, financial adjustments, and closure entries.

PPRL Record

Custom Purchase Price Review Line records capture bill-vs-PO variance state.

Approval Gate

Vendor Bill approval is blocked until the review is complete.

Rate Sync

Approved Rate to Pay is pushed into inbound shipment and item receipt rates.

Credit/Debit Logic

Bill approval creates vendor credit or overage vendor bill flows when needed.

JE Closure

Closed or fully billed PO lines trigger variance-closing journal entries.

Object Metadata

Field, list, record, and tab XML show the full package footprint.
